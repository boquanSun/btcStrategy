4.1.1 The function to calcuate basis based on future swap and perpetual swap

Calculate the % basis by (future - perpetual) / perpetual * 100

4.1.2 Open btc future swap datasets and store the basis dataframe into dictionary called futures. The key of futures dic is the delivery date of the contract

All the future datasets are in the dictionary called “futures” The “futures” dic key: the expiry date of the contract.
